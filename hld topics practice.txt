scalability -- it is the property of the system to handle a growing amount of load by adding resources to the system
a system that can adapt to the growing amount of work is called a scalable system

availability -- it is the proportion of time a system is operational and accessible when required.
availability == uptime / uptime + downtime
uptime -- the amount of time when the system is accessible
downtime -- the amount of time when the system is not accessible due to failures, or maintenance

latency -- it is the amount of time taken to complete 1 single request
low latency -- the system has less lag and is more responsive and has faster response time
high -- the system has more lag and the user will have bad ux

throughput -- it is the total amount of work done or data processed in a fixed amount of time
generally it is measured in requests per second (rps) or transactions per second (tps)
